First aid & Medical assistance information

In case of diving accident

Is the diver breathing? (look, listen, feel)

Yes
  • Check responsiveness: Non-responsive -> Recovery position
  • SAMPLE (Signs and Symptoms, Allergies, Medication, Previous history, Last meal, Events): Gain understanding
  • Assess for physical injuries: Treat if possible
  • Assess for medical shock: Weak pulse, pale -> Treat for shock
  • Assess for decompression illness: Skin-rash/chest or joint pain -> Recovery position and administer oxygene
  • Assess for heat stroke: Remove clothing, cool down
  • Assess for hypothermia: Blue lips/fingers/pale/weak pulse,cold to touch -> Warm up, give warm liquids
  • Administer Oxygen if necessary, monitor
No
  • Open airways
  • Administer rescue breaths
  • Begin CPR (30 compressions/2 breaths)
  • Check for pulse and breathing
  • Repeat until breathing or medical help available
  • Once restored re-evalute from top
Record any information (Dive site, time, names, equipment used, events, dive computer details) for medical personel. Vital to not modify equipment, do not disassemble. Create accident report for authorities.
